About Us

We believe every student deserves to be heard.

Student Mind Circle was founded by students who recognized a gap — between the pressures of academic life and the mental health support available. We exist to bridge that gap through awareness, peer support, and evidence-based research.

Our mission is to create a campus culture where talking about mental health is as normal as talking about academics — where no student feels alone, and every voice matters.
